Free eggs Get Crazy About Eggs too! Crazy About Eggs helps organic farmers to become fully organic circular. Every egg sold contributes to a better chicken life on the farm and a sustainable world for all of us.

Free-spirited chickens A life you would want if you were a chicken. With good health and enough sleep. The hens feel safe on higher perches and lay their biological eggs in a common nest.

Happy farmers Our organic and circular farmers receive a higher price for their eggs. In this way every egg sold helps them to become fully organic circular. That is good for the chickens, good for the farmer and good for our planet.
